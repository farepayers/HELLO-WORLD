This is an online map of the locations of ticket barriers on London's train network.

We assist fair payment of tickets and fines by mapping fare paying infrastructure.

Use this map and share it with your friends!

The info on this map is crowdsourced from the public.

If you know a station that has ticket barriers, or one that doesn't, you can text it to +44 7404 489 563 and we will update the map.
